Mike’s Receives Best Commercial Entry at the Pismo Clam Parade

2017-10-21 :: Pismo Beach, CA

Barber Zee accepts the trophy for best commercial entry at the Pismo Beach Clam Festival Parade! This was Mike’s Barber Shop’s first year participating in the exciting Pismo Beach parade. With Barber Zee, Jon, Jerry, Tahiti John, and friends and family marching alongside, it was a fun, festive event! Peace, love and friendship was the theme.

Mike’s Fade and Flat Top Clinic at Twig & Arrow Salon

2017-08-14 :: San Luis Obispo, CA

Barber Jon and Barber Ray led a fade and flat top clinic at Twig & Arrow Salon in San Luis Obispo this evening. It was quite a gathering as Mike’s 9 barbers were in attendance as well as Twig & Arrow cosmos and stylists and one adorable French Bulldog puppy.

Mike's in the SLO Tribune

The San Luis Obispo Tribune featured Mike's Barber Shop in today's Local section! The article highlights the 75th anniversary and history of the first Mike’s Barber Shop, located in downtown Morro Bay.

Mike's Opens in SLO

Mike’s is excited to open doors to the San Luis Obispo community with the new shop located at 1033 Chorro Street, street level to the BarrelHouse Speakeasy. A prohibition era theme brings 1920's antiques and barber chairs for nostalgic fun. Barber Jon and Barber Ray will provide services initially, with the addition of more barbers in the future. The shop will be open late on most days, come see us in SLO!
